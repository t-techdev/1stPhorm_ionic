Help ▸ Portion Control

This is a portion control diet plan. For maximum results, at each meal choose one protein (palm sized serving), one carb (fist sized serving), and one veggie (fist size serving). The Palm, Fist, Fist plan is easy and extremely effective!

Portion control is considered "Phase 1" when it comes to nutrition. It is designed to get you used to sizing up and eating correct portion sizes. It's a life-long skill that you will find yourself falling back to over and over again.
